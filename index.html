About Our Institution

Established in 2016, College of Education has been at the forefront of teacher education, combining traditional values with modern pedagogy.

Excellence in Teacher Education

Located in Erode, Tamil Nadu, College of Education is a premier B.Ed institution dedicated to producing highly competent, ethical, and innovative teachers who can shape the future of our nation.

Our 4-acre campus provides a serene learning environment with state-of-the-art facilities that foster academic excellence and holistic development.

We believe in education that goes beyond textbooks - nurturing critical thinking, creativity, and social responsibility in every student-teacher.
